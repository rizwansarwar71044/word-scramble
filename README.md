# Word Scramble

Welcome to the official repository for *Word Scramble*, a fun and addictive word puzzle game!

![Word Scramble App Icon](http://googleusercontent.com/image_generation_content/45a8df2d-dce7-440a-9d97-15104bebc63b)

## About the Game

Word Scramble is an engaging word puzzle game where you can challenge your brain by unscrambling jumbled letters to form meaningful words. Test your vocabulary and general knowledge skills across hundreds of exciting levels!

## Features

- *Challenging Levels:* Hundreds of puzzles to solve with increasing difficulty.
- *Score & Streaks:* Track your current score and maintain your solving streak to become a word master.
- *Hints:* Stuck on a word? Use hints to help you solve the puzzle.
- *Sleek UI:* A modern, dark-themed interface for an uninterrupted and pleasant gaming experience.
